About Me

I am an undergraduate in Data Science and Big Data Technology at Shenzhen Technology University. Concurrently, as a student in the joint TEEP program between Tsinghua University and Shenzhen X-Institute, I am engaged in advanced robotic gripper research focusing on **underactuated mechanisms** and **novel linkage-based** designs for constrained-space manipulation. My goal is to develop highly efficient and adaptive robotic systems for complex real-world tasks.
